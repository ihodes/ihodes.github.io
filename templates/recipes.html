Chinese Medicinal Soup

Ingredients

  • Red dates（紅棗) - pitted and halved
  • herbs - to be added to hot water
  • 肉 (Your choice of meat / ròu) - chicken, pork, or beef
  • Shaoxing wine（紹興酒) - 2/3 bottle
  • Goji berries （狗機子)
  • Salt, to taste

Preparation

  1. Wash all dry ingredients thoroughly and let them dry
  2. Cut red dates in half and remove the pits
  3. Cut other herb ingredients into halves or thirds as needed

Cooking Instructions

  1. Add all dried herbs (except goji berries, Shaoxing wine, and meat) to a large pot hot water
  2. Bring the pot to a boil over medium-high heat
  3. Once boiling, add your choice of meat to the pot
  4. Reduce heat to a simmer and cook for 40 minutes
  5. Pour in 1/3 bottle of Shaoxing wine
  6. Continue simmering for another 30 minutes
  7. Add goji berries and simmer for an additional 20 minutes
  8. Season with salt to taste before serving

Total cooking time: approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes
