Other people's places online

A non-exhaustive list of people who inspire and impress me, and their places online.

  • Dan Wang - annual letters are incredible
  • Matt Levine - perhaps, one of the best newsletters of all time
  • Kevin Lynagh - builds for the sake of it
  • Patrick McKenzie - writing about finance
  • Cedric Chin - writing about how to thoughtfully do business
  • Craig Mod - writing & walking, thoughtful observer
  • Jungwon Byun - actively building tools to improve research and thought
  • Andy Matuschak - researching how to learn better
  • Linus Lee - working on tools to help write to think
  • Bret Victor - the one and only
  • Casey Handmer - physicist, building things to fix the world
  • Geoffrey Litt - improving computing
  • Michael Nielsen - physicist, thinking about learning and research acceleration
  • Amelia Wattenberger - design, computing, representation
  • Ed Yong - science journalist, which undersells him greatly
  • Kanjun Qiu - studies people, institutions, and cultures
  • Ralph Ammer - illustrator, essayist, creativity and philosophy
  • Patrick Collison - cofounder Stripe, thoughtful technologist, helping others build the future
  • Jason Crawford - establishing a new philosophy of progress
  • Nat Friedman - cofounder GitHub, thoughtful technologist and writer
  • Milan Cvitkovic - FRO innovator, now working on brain science
  • Greg Gundersen - writing to learn math online, PhD making the complex profitable
  • Ben Kuhn - thoughtful engineer and writer about learning and understanding
  • Will Larson - thoughtful technology manager and writer on topics of organizations and management
  • Maxim Zaslavsky - PhD at the intersection of computers and immunology
  • Sebastian Claici - ML researcher and Renaissance man
